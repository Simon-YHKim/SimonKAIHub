Verdict (synthesis after cluster)

Low-pressure personal record ownership + quiet permanence framing ("this is mine and will still be here / for future me") is the single most reproducible, high-leverage consumer signal for week-1 survival, habit formation, and addressing the first-glimpse feature gap. It outperforms streak/gamify pressure, daily quotas, AI-nag, and abstract "second brain" positioning on both retention and authentic language. Pressure framing triggers reactance and early churn; autonomy + enduring ownership builds quiet pride and low-friction return visits.
