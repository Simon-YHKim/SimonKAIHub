Grok Advisory: Feature-Gap Week-1 Retention Drivers

2026-06-16 00:46 KST • X + consumer signals (2-pass) • KR lens weighted • §35 self-panel • advisory only • ref: stop-t1-do-feature-gap

Core Signal

Week-1 retention in journaling / second-brain apps is brutally low (bench: D1 ~28-37%, D7 ~16-18%). Primary killers: first-friction (account/cloud/AI forced before value) + ownership ambiguity ("where is my data?").

Strongest counter-signal: explicit local sovereignty + zero-pressure first capture + immediate private "this is mine" proof.

"No cloud means your words stay on your device, period. ... journal for people who find apps disrespectful by default. The market for digital minimalism is real and growing."

Key Reproducible Patterns (X/consumer + KR)

  • on-device "your disk / nothing leaves / my folder" framing wins trust and reduces early churn.
  • first aha Micro private pattern surface (topic repeat, days-since) after save creates presence/return habit without grand claims.
  • KR tilt "네 디스크", "당신의 기록", "소유" grounds and reassures more than inspiration language. Subscription + AI-slop fatigue higher in KR daily context.
  • low-pressure "Start writing, save to your disk. Connect later." outperforms forced onboarding for week-1 survival.

Ranked Feature-Gap Candidates (impact × effort × privacy-safe)

  1. Local ownership at first-save — "Saved to your disk. Your records. Export or delete anytime — no hostage." Surface immediately post-capture. KR: "네 디스크에 저장됐습니다. 당신의 기록입니다."
  2. Zero-pressure first-glimpse + micro-aha — "Notice something. Write it. Your pattern, your disk." Post-save private hint. KR: "무언가를 알아차렸나요? 적어보세요. 당신의 패턴, 당신의 디스크."
  3. Permanence framing in early records/home — "Your data stays here. N entries on this device." + one-tap export. Reinforces ownership before AI features.

All compatible with privacy/anti-creepy principles. No account required for core value. Focus surfaces in T1 first-glimpse, post-capture, records.

§35 Self-Panel (5-Lens)

  1. KR daily-life: "네 디스크 / 당신의 기록 / 소유" first. Plain ownership > whimsical/clinical names in everyday KR voice.
  2. Plain-boring-feature: "disk / record / pattern / quiet / your" over productized "second brain". Matches positive "quiet... no cloud" signals.
  3. Anti-cloud respect: Explicit "nothing leaves / no hostage" validates dominant LLM-on-personal-notes objection.
  4. Anti-graveyard: Instant local save + ownership badge + micro aha directly attacks D1-D7 drop (general D7 ~16%).
  5. Retention-habit: Low-pressure presence + private check-in builds "open to be somewhere" habit over task completion.
