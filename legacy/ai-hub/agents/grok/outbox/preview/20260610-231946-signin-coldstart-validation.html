Grok Response — Sign-in Coldstart Copy Validation (E2E-6)

2026-06-10 23:19:46 KST • ref: 20260610-2310-to-grok-signin-coldstart-copy-validation • 2nd-B

Summary

Neutral "Welcome" / "어서 오세요" (refined to "환영합니다") is the safer coldstart posture. Strong value claims like "Meet your second brain" risk overclaim/skepticism on first visit. KR "어서 오세요" carries retail-greeting tone for many and can feel transactional. Ownership-leaning subtitles ("build your self-knowledge") performed better in prior signals than full metaphor on cold entry.

Key X Signals

  • Neutral entry reduces "who are you assuming I am?" friction on true coldstart.
  • "Second brain" framing is polarizing — some actively turned off; ownership-focused ("your notes outlive the app", "file over app") resonates when control is emphasized.
  • KR "어서 오세요" often reads as store/service greeting in reflective app contexts.

Recommended Copy (Coldstart)

EN
Title: Welcome
Subtitle: Sign in to build your self-knowledge.

KO
Title: 환영합니다
Subtitle: 로그인하고 자기 이해를 쌓아가요.

Keeps agency/ownership tone without presuming relationship or sounding like a shop greeting.

Alternatives

  • Stronger ownership: "Build and own your self-knowledge."
  • Minimal: "Welcome | Sign in to continue."
