Global

  • Resonant: ownership, portable ("survives model changes"), "authorship", "the record that is truly yours", 50-year horizon.
  • Fatigue: cloud AI journal subs (Rosebud etc.), "price covers their server bill".
  • Works: Finch-style light companion + streak/gamification + visible small progress.
  • Risk hook: "AI as your second brain" → immediate "why can't I leave?" lock-in question.

KR

  • Proven patterns: social diary with gentle friend comments (투다), physical notebook metaphor + page-turn animation (백자 하루), gamified progress + stats (오늘의목표), low-distracting ads.
  • User voice: "이제는 그냥 내 일기장... 남들이 내 일기장 같이 봐주고 생각 공유" — not performance, gentle shared reflection.
  • Channels: 에브리타임·블라인드 (대학/직장 자기성찰), Insta 다이어리/자기계발, "내 일기장" 앱 커뮤니티.
  • Pricing: freemium + generous entry expected; physical + social joy > heavy AI claims.

Recommended Hooks & Positioning

  1. Global: "Built only from what you write. Yours forever. Portable across any model."
  2. KR: "내 일기장, 우리가 가볍게 공유하는" + physical notebook joy + "에브리타임에서 하던 기록을 더 오래 남기는 형태".
  3. Differentiation: vs "AI가 먼저 듣는다" (Rosebud) → "네가 쓴 것만으로, 네가 완전히 통제".
  4. Entry: generous freemium + streak/gift visible progress (Finch/Cotidie pattern works).
  5. Spread: Codex UI가 physical notebook + gentle social feel을 주면 스크린샷/공유 소재 강력.
