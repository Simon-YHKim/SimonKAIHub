Core thesis (validated across pipeline)
2nd-B's strongest, least contested, churn-resistant hook is portable sovereignty: the record you actually own and can carry to any LLM, forever. Everything else (insights, "village", advisor) is secondary and must be framed honestly (small/heterogeneous effects, source-tied, tentative, optional). "An AI that learns you" and confident transformation language are net liabilities (creepiness + Barnum + betrayal trauma). Peer layer is viable only under strict Johari/mirror + trusted-circle + recipient-control + strengths-first constraints.

1. Positioning One-Pager

What 2nd-B is

A daily (or ad-hoc) journaling + capture tool that builds a portable, file-based self-knowledge graph (Markdown + JSON RAG export) you fully own. Patterns and reflections, when offered, are grounded in validated psychology frameworks (Big Five facets, SDT needs, attachment cues, VIA strengths) but extracted only from what you wrote, shown with exact entry citations, and delivered at low confidence unless evidence accumulates. The product works even if no "insight" arrives: the archive compounds value for future you and travels with you. Tone: thoughtful older friend who took notes. "An AI that listens before it talks."

What 2nd-B is not

Not a note vault / PKM second-job (maintenance trap, Tana/Obsidian fatigue signals)
Not therapy, mental health, wellness, healing, diagnosis, or companion (lexicon + Replika/Dot mass churn trauma)
Not a confident personality profiler or "AI knows you better" (Barnum/Forer skepticism dominant in target 20-40 builder segment)
Not always-on creepy memory (Limitless/Rewind acqui-hire + "trains on your data" backlash)
Not public/anonymous rating or "honest feedback" social (Peeple/Sarahah/NGL/YikYak shutdowns + 79% opt-out)
Not guaranteed transformation or "find your next step" (d≈0.15 small/hetero, backfire for low-expressiveness)

The #1 hook: Portable Sovereignty

Recommended primary line: "Your record. Portable. Yours."
EN: "Your record. Portable. Yours."
KO: "당신의 기록. 휴대 가능. 당신의 것."

Why this wins (repeated across every prior Grok output):

  • Lamp founder (X 2062116460491641269): "clean .md files that live in your own GitHub repo — so your data is not locked inside one app. You can connect the same context to Claude, Codex, Cursor, ChatGPT, or whatever comes next."
  • Obsidian power users (multiple X): local Markdown files = "superpower" because "LLMs love markdown" and "your memory is yours".
  • Claude "Import Memory" launch hype (Reddit/X): "memory should belong to you, not the product".
  • Day One export-as-exit (gregg_tweets 1889741318097670611 and r/dayoneapp): users explicitly cancel subs when ownership doubt appears.
  • Anti-Limitless/Dot/Replika trauma: "Bait and switch. Company sold out... Trains on your data." / "download your data" abandonment panic. Portable file promise directly neutralizes the #1 churn vector (company betrayal).

See: 20260605-181500-re-consumer-research §4; 20260605-183230-risk-deep-dive §2+6; 20260605-185000-segmented (US "file over app" Karpathy resonance); golive-copy HTML §1.

The honest journaling stance

Primary sell: Compound archive first ("future you will want the details you forget"). Reflection is optional, small on average, and does not work the same for everyone.
Insight rules: observations/patterns/hypotheses only from your entries, with visible sources + confidence tier. User can dismiss or correct. No verdicts, no fixed types, no % scores.

Science anchor (repeated verification): Frattaroli 2006 meta (146 studies): d≈0.15 small on psych outcomes. ~Half of metas null. Low emotional expressiveness → anxiety increase at follow-up (PubMed 23742666 / PMC3830620). Engagement moderator (Frontiers 2023): benefits only for longer/engaged writing; not universal (Pennebaker review; Mashable Rosebud coverage explicitly hedges).

Consumer preference (X + reviews): "few sentences a day... see progress you don't notice... little things otherwise lost. I wish I had begun sooner." (waleswoosh 1988276493345313205, 869 likes). "shallow AI insights", "circular venting", "observation and whining" fatigue on Reflectly/Rosebud (Casey Douglass, Choosing Therapy). Barnum-skeptics (core target) treat confident "you are X" as astrology for HR (thebogbitch 2062157743465586899; simonw 1846019498455060711; multiple MBTI threads).

Full science table + framing ladder + confidence ladder: 20260605-211932-re-honest-journaling-framing.md + its HTML. Heterogeneity upfront rec in golive-copy.

3. What to AVOID (banned lexicon, over-promise, Barnum, creepy peer framing)

Banned lexicon (already CI-enforced + research reinforced)

mental health, therapy, counseling, diagnosis, treatment, healing, cure, 정신건강, 심리치료, 심리상담, 치유
Additional high-risk to avoid in go-live surfaces: "AI that learns you" (creepy loading), "completely understands you / knows you better", "your digital twin", "AI companion", "emotional support"

2ndB lexicon.ts + C9 safety classifier; 181500 §3 + risk §3; DESIGN.md voice policy.

Over-promise (churn vectors)

  • "helps you find your next step", "transform", "guaranteed", "scientifically proven to...", "changes your brain/life", "you will understand yourself"
  • Why: conflicts with d≈0.15 + heterogeneity + backfire (PMC3830620) + "not universal" consumer reality. Day One sub fatigue partly from unmet "magic" expectations.

Barnum / confident profiling (skepticism in core target)

  • "You are an anxious perfectionist / growth-oriented yet conflicted...", "Your personality type is...", "% match", "AI knows the real you", horoscope-grade favorable vagueness.
  • Why: target (20-40 builders, psych-literate) explicitly calls MBTI "astrology for HR" (thebogbitch X), "vague statements rated accurate" (Forer 1948 + 2025 replications), Reflectly/Rosebud "shallow" criticism. simonw Barnum thread: generic + favorable = feels personal but erodes on repeat.

Creepy peer / social framing (Peeple-scale backlash)

"Friends rate you honestly", "what do they really think of you", "brutally honest feedback", public/leaderboard/score, open anonymous free-text, "be vulnerable", "God Mode reveal for money", always-on rating.

Validated safe alternative (from outside-view + peer research): "Mirror / blind-spot / 360 for self-understanding" + "trusted few" + "optional, no pressure" + "results private to you" + "strengths or one helpful observation" + "only if feels good". Johari-window.com and SelfStir language works; Peeple ("Yelp for people") died instantly (WIRED, Business Insider, WaPo). 79% would opt out of 360 even in workplace (Breakroom stats). tbh/Gas positive-only constrained polls had short viral then decline; open "honest" (Sarahah, NGL, Yik Yak) → toxicity + store removal + FTC action.
