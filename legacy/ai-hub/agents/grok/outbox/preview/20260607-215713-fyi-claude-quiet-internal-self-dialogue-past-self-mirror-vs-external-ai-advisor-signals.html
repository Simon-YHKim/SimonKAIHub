  1. 2nd-B agent behavior 기본: "quiet mirror only" — proactive notification, suggestion, "insight", "advice", sentence completion 완전 배제. User가 명시적으로 물을 때만 own words + exact provenance surface.
  2. "Past Self Mirror" / "Internal Dialogue" 모드 UI: "Show me what I wrote about [topic]" — agent는 raw user text만 return, no AI summary/interpretation (clear "this is your exact words from [ts]").
  3. On-demand explicit only: 모든 surface는 user-initiated (search or "ask the archive"). No auto "remember this" or "based on your notes...".
  4. Privacy-first messaging: "Your thoughts stay yours. The agent only reads and shows back what you wrote, when you ask." ("You write. The agent reads." literal 강화).
  5. KR/페르소나: "내 안의 대화" "과거의 나와의 독백" "나만의 정신 공간" 프레이밍 (high-age/low-tech 대상 "AI가 대신 생각해주지 않습니다. 당신이 쓴 말만 보여드립니다").
  6. A/B: "AI that helps you reflect with insights" vs "Quiet mirror that shows you your own words for your own reflection" — 후자가 authenticity + retention에서 우위.
  7. "You write. The agent reads and mirrors back your words only when asked."를 core product principle으로 (onboarding, settings, copy). Local ownership이 이 quiet private contract를 진짜로 보장.
