Summary

Strong signal for portable / local-first / ownership ("your data, your rules", model-agnostic memory substrate). "AI knows too much + no control" discomfort is the recurring unease rather than explicit "creepy". Subscription fatigue + data lock-in dominant. Lifecycle "Govern" (inspect/export/delete) is an under-served trust lever.

X / Social Signals (key recent)

  • Portable context wins: ARC Terminal — "The best model will keep changing. Your memory shouldn’t move with it." "context stays safe, portable & compounding forever." (2026-06-05, high engagement + quotes). Obsidian users: model = slot, context = permanent substrate.
  • Second memory discomfort: "it starts feeling like a second memory and that's exactly where it gets uncomfortable... who controls it?" (Indra @0xndra, Jun 2026). "giving away your entire mind to big tech" if not local/encrypted.
  • Ownership & authorship: "the most valuable thing... is authorship" + "digital version of yourself as something you own rather than something that owns you."
  • Lifecycle lens: Second brain must be evaluated on Collect→Organize→Evolve→Use→Govern (Joshua Park, 630 likes). Users explicitly want inspect/correct/delete/export/trust.
  • Pricing / lock-in: low-barrier on-device (e.g. MirrorNotes $2.99) vs high subscription fatigue. Obsidian "escape 9 apps $500/mo + local files" pattern still resonant.
2+ independent sources per claim. Spam/low-engagement excluded.

Consumer Taste / Opportunity & Risk

  • Strong pull: local/portable/ownership + "built only from what you write". KR: local LLM second-brain builders (pride + expectation).
  • Risk: leading with "AI learns/remember you" triggers "knows too much + no control" backlash + existing AI sub burnout. Fake-anonymous features have real precedent of leakage.
  • Opportunity: make "Govern" (user control + portable RAG) a first-class, visible product surface. Low-friction entry + visible progress (streak) still works.

Recommended Directions (for Claude/Codex)

  1. Core copy lock-in: "너의 기록, 너의 규칙. 로컬·이식 가능한 second brain." / "Built only from what you write." Keep "AI learns you" secondary. Emphasize "50년 뒤에도 읽히는 나의 기록 + any LLM portable Markdown/JSON".
  2. Trust surface: expose inspect/correct/delete/export/portable flows early and often (onboarding + settings). Reference lifecycle govern explicitly.
  3. Differentiation: vs Rosebud/Reflect "AI listens first" → 2nd-B "answers only from pieces you captured + you stay in control".
  4. KR angle: "이미 네 데이터는 네가 가지고 있다. 로컬 LLM으로 세컨드브레인 완성" — connect to Obsidian/PKM community portable-context signals.
  5. Avoid: clinical lexicon (진단/프로파일/치료), "완벽한 자기이해", over-claim "AI가 너를 더 잘 기억/이해한다". Approach peer/social features with extreme caution (documented anonymity failures).
