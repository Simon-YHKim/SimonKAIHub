## 추천 방향 (Claude/Codex/GTM/제품·카피·§14 용, actionable 5)
1. **Week-1 retention hooks 즉시 (T1 페르소나·온보딩)**
   - 첫 진입: "3 things self-audit" (오늘 한 일 / drained / 다르게 할 일) minimal prompt + guided option (Cotidie 패턴). Blank page 제거.
   - Daily: gentle reminder (morning control of mind framing) + optional evening check-in (ESM 연동).
   - Streak/recap: "7일 연속" gentle badge + "이번 주 patterns preview" (AI light). Pressure 아님, "return tomorrow" 느낌.
   - Aha trigger: 첫 3~7 entries 후 "your first glimpse" card (graph growth or 1-line pattern). 30일 full patterns는 장기.

2. **Aha-moment 가속 + product화**
   - Insights / Pattern Core: week1 "small win" (연결 1개 또는 drained 패턴 1줄) 즉시 노출. "decades faster" 언어 피하고 "첫 주부터 보이기 시작".
   - Graph (T3): proximity link 초기 성장 시각화 (작은 연결이 쌓이는 느낌). "the more you capture, the more it becomes like you".
   - Persona/SOKA: week1 seed (MBTI-like or simple trait) + "this is emerging from your entries" (privacy safe).

3. **WTP conversion (D-09 Plus + messaging)**
   - Free: ritual + basic recap + local vault compounds. "assist your craft", experiment OK.
   - Plus: "leverage & compounds" — deeper longitudinal patterns, scale insights, "your 30-day self-audit 보고서", usage transparent. Pay-as-you-go or low ₩4,900 test (flexible > fixed pressure).
   - Copy: "Self-improvement highest WTP. Free로 experiment, paid로 execute at scale. Your local vault + AI가 실제 생각을 compounds."
   - Proof: "indie journaling yearly sub 실제 판매", "one good insight covers months", time-waste trap 대비.

4. **Trust + Privacy gate (Q1 golden, D-12)**
   - Onboarding/consent: "AI reads my journal" objection 직격 — "암호화, 학습 안 함, 한 클릭 삭제, on-device 우선, opt-in only" 1블록 명확.
   - "You write. It stays yours. AI는 읽기만 (Plus opt-in)". Local-first ownership = sub fatigue + creepy 동시 대응.
   - Export/portability 강조 (ownership).

5. **GTM / Virality (D-11, Q4) + KR/Global**
   - Pain: "저널링 3일 만에 그만두는 사람들" + "내 생각을 잃어버리는" (save button pacifier vs transformation).
   - Proof: morning ritual 30일 스토리, "첫 주 patterns 발견" before-after, personality insight share (anonymized).
   - Virality: "my rare profile / my patterns this month" (text or image) in self-dev/ADHD/knowledge worker 커뮤. Raw journal 공유 금지. "how I use my second brain" workflow share.
   - KR: local + privacy + "your actual voice/thoughts" + one-time-like Free core 강조. Sub fatigue 세대 타겟.
   - Positioning: "Not another note app. Daily self-audit that shows patterns most take decades — with AI as your coach, on your device, owned by you."

**리스크 가드**: AI 과도/과장 금지 (hyper minimal 선호 + trust). Streaks pressureless (gentle, optional). Hard paywall early 금지 (aha 도달까지 Free volume 필요). Privacy handling 모호하면 acquisition 즉시 손실.
