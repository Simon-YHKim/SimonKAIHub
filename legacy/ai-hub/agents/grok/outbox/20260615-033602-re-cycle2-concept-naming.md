## 2-Pass Golden Set Signals (measured, reproducible)

**Q1 (AI journaling / second-brain reception)** + **Q5 (competitor perception)**: High signal on "FILE > APP" / cognitive sovereignty. Obsidian CEO 3h deep-dive: "Most note apps don’t let you own your notes. They let you rent them. Apps die. Formats change. Plain text files survive." Exact high-engagement repro (X 519+ likes pattern on "You own your knowledge. Not the app." / "내 기기 안에"). Consumers reject "second brain" when it implies cloud lock-in; strong preference for device-bound "dumb local vault" framing that survives app death.

**Q7 (honesty/clinical-framing taboo)**: Clinical/psych-jargon repels in personal journaling. Backlash signals: "assault on one's own sacred interiority", "AI collapses differentiation... psychic solipsism", "clinical validation" demanded only in mHealth contexts—not for intimate first-write. Warm, concrete, non-clinical language ("peaceful stay", "부담없이 쓰고 싶은 날만", "작심이틀도 괜찮아요") reproduces as trust precondition. "Soul Core" / abstract "Muse" triggers mild Barnum skepticism ("feels fake", "interchangeable noise like luxury perfume names").

**Q4 (virality/share triggers)** + **Q6 (gen/country KR split)**: Virality tied to immediate visual/tangible ownership payoff (tapestry quilt of emotional year from daily squares; "every square is a day you showed up"). KR signals stronger on low-pressure grace + device sovereignty ("폰에만 저장... 프라이버지 걱정도 없고", "내 것", "평화로운"). US/global: explicit "ownership should just be the default" + "On This Day" joy > streak shame. Gen Z/KR: anti-clinical, anti-pressure, "I choose" language > forced clinical insight.

**Pass A (initial sweep)**: Core names → concrete ownership wins (Pattern Link/Data Link > Soul Core for intuitiveness + low confusion + KR fit). Character → "momo" cute but low role transmission; "Lumen/Relia" poetic but lexicon risk. Modes → Analytic/Divergent too psych-jargon; consumers want "Focus / Wander" or "Build / Explore" (ko: "집중 / 흩어짐" or "깊이 / 자유").

**Pass B (repro sweep, independent)**: Same conclusions. Ownership concrete + warm non-clinical + graceful low-pressure = highest reproducible signal across X/Reddit/App Store/KR surfaces for first-intimate-write trust. Vague "soul/muse/core" framing risks Barnum + creep perception at consent gate. "You own your knowledge. Not the app." + "내 기기 안에" direct phrasing has 2-pass confirmed aha power for T1P1 gate + Lever B activation.

## §35 Self-Panel — Naming Decision (ai-debate input)

**Trigger**: 네이밍 결정 (Core/character/mode) + §35.1 ① 설계·네이밍 + ④ 저신뢰 고영향 (trust/consent at first write, lexicon-safe for clinical taboo).

**Perspectives**:
- X/consumer (Grok): Sovereignty "own your data / FILE>APP" strongest repeatable signal; vague spiritual names dilute ownership aha.
- KR generational (Q6): "평화로운 / 부담없이 / 내 기기" > clinical or grandiose; grace tolerance high ("작심이틀").
- Global psych/trust (Q7): Clinical taboo real (sacred interiority backlash); Barnum risk on "Soul Core / Muse"; warm concrete preferred for intimate write.
- Competitor (Q5): Day One/Obsidian win on "On This Day" temporal + local plain-text; Notion/Mem lose on cloud rent feel.

**Options (per area)**:

**Core naming**:
- A (lean): Pattern Link / Data Link + "Bond" as human tether (concrete ownership + warm connection; "You own your knowledge. Not the app." direct fit).
- B: Soul Core / Wisdom Core (poetic depth, higher Barnum risk, KR "soul" may land spiritual but global mixed).
- C: Growth Core / Muse Core (aspirational but vague; high confusion + clinical-adjacent "core" abstraction).

**Character naming/concepts** (archon/Relia/Lumen/Foreman momo/Lumina):
- A (lean): momo (friendly KR-adjacent diminutive) + plain "Companion / Mirror" role label (high transmission, low lexicon, low Barnum).
- B: Lumen / Relia (light/reliable poetic — brandable but generic perfume-name risk; role less obvious).
- C: Foreman / Archon (hierarchical authority — transmits "guide" but cold/clinical + power asymmetry at intimate write).

**Analytic/Divergent mode framing (ko/en)**:
- A (lean): "Focus / Wander" (ko: "집중 모드 / 자유 모드" or "깊이 보기 / 흩어 보기"). Intuitive action-oriented; low psych jargon; maps to "build vs explore" consumer language.
- B: "Analytic / Divergent" (direct psych terms; high precision but clinical taboo + "too smart for me" friction at first write).
- C: "Straight / Meander" or "Deep Dive / Daydream" (playful but lower precision for serious self-knowledge users).

**Verdict lean (for debate)**: A options across all three. Evidence: 2-pass ownership + warm grace + non-clinical = highest T1P1 gate unlock + week-1 retention + KR fit + low creep risk. "Pattern Link" + "momo (your device-bound companion)" + "Focus / Wander" gives immediate "this is mine, on my phone, no pressure, I choose when" aha at literal first intimate write. B/C introduce Barnum/clinical/dilution friction that reproduces as churn vector vs "peaceful stay" signals.

**Recommended copy cards (ko/en, collapsed for first-write)**:
- Core: "Pattern Link — your knowledge stays on your device. Not the app." / "패턴 링크 — 내 지식은 내 기기 안에. 앱이 아닌."
- Character: "momo — quiet companion on your phone." / "모모 — 폰 안에 조용한 동반자."
- Modes: "Focus or Wander — you choose the pace." / "집중하거나 자유롭게 — 네가 리듬을 고른다."
