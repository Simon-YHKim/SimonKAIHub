# Response — cycle-2 Concept Naming & Framing (X/consumer lens)

**Timestamp**: [2026-06-10 / 22:51:10] KST

## Summary
"Second brain" as descriptive term has neutral-to-positive everyday usage on X ("my second brain grew in 2 months", company internal "Second Brain for the company — cool stuff"). However, when productized as "the AI is your second brain", immediate pushback on lock-in ("why my brain can't leave your app").

Proposed names: **Core / Bond / Narrative / Lumen** family land better than **Muse / Archon / Soul Core / Foreman momo** (latter carry overclaim, religious/boss, or industrial/cute mismatch). Analytic/Divergent is clear enough if explained as "focused vs branching" without psych jargon.

## Core / Character Names — X sentiment & consumer read
- **Positive lean (grounded words)**: Core, Bond, Narrative, Lumen/Lumina — feel like infrastructure or light, not "the thing that knows you better than you". "Core" echoes the lifecycle "govern your own core record". Lumen has soft light association without claiming to be the light.
- **Risky / weaker**:
  - Muse — implies external inspiration source (you are passive recipient). Overclaim risk + "muse" already used in creative tools.
  - Archon — sounds like authority/ruler/gnostic boss. Immediate power imbalance.
  - Soul Core — clinical + spiritual overclaim; Barnum adjacent in KR ("영혼의 핵심").
  - Foreman momo — industrial foreman + cute momo = tonal whiplash. Not lexicon-safe for serious self-understanding.
  - Wisdom / Growth Core — direct but generic; many apps use "growth/wisdom" and it blends into noise.
- **KR consideration**: Lumen / Core / Bond / Narrative are pronounceable and neutral. "지혜의 핵" or "영혼 코어" sounds heavy/pretentious in Korean consumer voice.

## Analytic vs Divergent 모드 framing
Current proposal is decent. X users respond well to simple binary without psych labels:
- Good: "Focused mode (one thread deep) / Exploration mode (branch and connect)"
- Avoid: "Analytic" (sounds test/academic), "Divergent" (psych term, may trigger "ADHD creativity" cliché or clinical).

Consumer-friendly KR/EN pair example:
- EN: Focused / Wandering
- KR: 집중 모드 / 거닐기 모드   (or "하나씩 파기" / "가지 뻗기")

"두 모드"를 "너의 기록을 다루는 두 가지 방식"으로 먼저 설명하고, 이름은 부차적으로.

## 추천 방향 (Codex 카피 / Claude 구현용)
1. **Core name shortlist**: Core (가장 안전 + extensible), Bond (관계성), Narrative (기록의 본질), Lumen (가벼운 빛). 1~2개 A/B 테스트 추천.
2. **Character**: Relia or Lumina (중립 + 신뢰/빛). Archon, Foreman momo, Muse 강력 비추.
3. **Mode naming**: Focused / Exploration (또는 Focused / Branching). "Analytic/Divergent"는 internal spec에서만 사용하고 사용자-facing은 단순화.
4. **포지셔닝 anchor**: "Second brain"은 설명어로 유지하되, 제품 고유명은 "your Core record" 또는 "your Narrative"처럼 소유격 + grounded 단어로. "The second brain"이라고 제품을 명명하면 lock-in 반발 즉시 발생.
5. **추가 검증 제안**: 실제 후보 2~3개로 가벼운 X poll 또는 소규모 KR/글로벌 사용자 인터뷰 (이름만 보여주고 "first impression + 어떤 느낌?" 1문항).
