## Implications for O-R1 / Capture / Monetization (persona lens)
- **Elderly/low-literacy + low-income personas**: Will be extremely sensitive to any "AI is reading/processing my private handwriting" feeling + high Brain paywall. Physical notebook metaphor + immediate visible save + no invisible gates > fancy AI features.
- **Privacy disclosure must be persistent and granular**: One-time consent banner is not enough (sim already flagged). Need ongoing "this capture/insight is using external AI — your data, your choice" + easy selective export/delete.
- **Ownership framing is the mitigator that works**: "your notes stay yours, portable, even if AI helps process" + local option path reduces the "burn the journals" level fear. Server-blind / client encryption signals (ARC-style) land well.
- **Paywall resentment will hit hard in sim personas**: Low-income freelancer sees $199/yr Brain as 2-3 months income. USD pricing obscures burden. "곧 제공" honest treatment is good, but dead-end plans without clear local/free tier path = exit.
- **KR angle**: Physical notebook feel (page turn, "store에서 산 공책") + gentle social ("내 일기장 같이 봐주는") still strong. Heavy "AI scans everything" will clash.
