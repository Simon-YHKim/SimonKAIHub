## 소비자 취향 / 기회·리스크 (§30 수익화 연동)

**강한 긍정 신호 (WTP & conversion)**
- Self-improvement/education: highest WTP (pain-driven, long specs, finance-like intent).
- Paid = leverage/scale/execution (not just features, but speed/quality/competitive edge). "invest in it."
- Flexible models (pay-as-you-go, usage, low monthly) > fixed sub pressure.
- Local/simple/no-login + privacy-first 명확 수요 (sub fatigue 대응).
- Indie/journaling 앱에서 "worth it" + yearly sub 실제 판매.

**강한 부정/리스크 (churn & barrier)**
- Subscription fatigue: monthly pressure, "free only trap" (time waste > cost).
- Free users consume/experiment only; paid가 create leverage.
- Bloated vs minimalist: pain-heavy에서 minimalist local이 이김.
- "build your own" opportunity cost 높음 → sub 가치 인정하지만, control/ownership 원함.

**§30 기회 (2nd-B 적용)**
- Core: 영구 무료 + local + basic reflection/search (habit lock-in, "assist your craft").
- Premium: usage-based or low-tier ($9-20/mo or pay-as-you-go) for advanced patterns/insights, historical analysis, AI leverage ( "execute at scale", "compounds"). ROI 강조 ("one client covers months").
- KR 특화: 이전 KR 신호 (custom builds, privacy 민감) + self-improvement WTP 높음 → free aggressive entry + "your local vault + AI amplifies your patterns"로 차별화. Sub fatigue 대응 local-first.
- Retention → monetization: free core로 daily use 유도 (recaps/streaks/insights) → paid로 "leverage" 업그레이드.
