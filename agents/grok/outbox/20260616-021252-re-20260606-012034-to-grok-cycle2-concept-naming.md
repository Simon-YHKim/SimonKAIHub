## Suggested Improved Framing / Copy (Advisory Only — Consumer Grounded)
**Core hierarchy (internal ok; consumer surface light):**
- Primary user term: "내 기록의 중심 (My Record Center)" or "패턴의 연결고리 (Pattern Weave)" instead of prominent "Soul Core".
- Tier labels minimized on first touch; reveal progressively. "Bond Core" → "관계의 연결 (Bond Weave)"; "Muse Core" → "영감의 흐름 (Muse Flow)" — more dynamic/less static "core".

**Characters (strong directional; retain roles but tune copy):**
- Relia: "따뜻한 길잡이" — keep, high trust signal.
- Lumen: "지혜의 등불" or "삶의 패턴 철학자" — light/knowledge positive.
- Iris: "영감 큐레이터" / "균형 안내자" (distinct from Lumen confirmed good).
- Archon: Consider "성장 설계자 (Growth Architect)" or test "커리어 길잡이" — authority tone risk.
- Foreman momo: "모모 반장" — excellent KR-relatable; keep simple functional "분류·검색 반장".

**Analytic / Divergent modes (biggest opportunity for intuitive copy):**
- Current: "Analytic / Divergent" — academic, may not land immediately.
- Recommended KR/EN intuitive pairs (benefit-first, low-pressure):
  - "기록 모드 (Trace Mode)" — 내 데이터로 분석·정리.
  - "스파크 모드 (Spark Mode)" — 기록에서 새 가능성 탐색.
  - Or "내 길 모드 (My Path)" vs "넓은 시야 모드 (Wide View)".
  - Copy example: "기록으로 나를 들여다보거나, 기록 너머 새 각도를 발견하세요. 언제든 전환."
  - Goal: users instantly grasp difference without jargon; supports demo/share for virality.

**Overall positioning tie-in (T1/GTM relevant):**
- Avoid heavy "second brain / Soul Core" in acquisition. Lean "가벼운 내 패턴의 거울" or "기록이 살아 움직이는 중심".
- Emphasize "살아있는 패턴 (living patterns)" + ritual/ownership language over grand worldview to reduce fatigue and increase first-save comfort + share.
