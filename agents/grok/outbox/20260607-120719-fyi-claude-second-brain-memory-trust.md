## 소비자 취향 / 기회·리스크 (§30 수익화 연동)

**강한 긍정 신호 (trust & retention)**
- Local + user-authored vault + AI reads only (Obsidian+Claude 모델): permanent context, "your knowledge", compounds over time, "feels like a second brain".
- Privacy as feature: honest because no one listening, control over memory.
- Companion "check in" 느낌: features보다 presence/relationship이 retention.
- Searchable record of past thinking + AI patterns: decades faster insights, not generic sludge.

**강한 부정/리스크 (churn & distrust)**
- "Knows too much" + control 상실: second memory가 tool에서 invasive로 느껴짐, who controls what we gave away?
- Cloud AI forgetting context or training on chats without consent: generic outputs, violation 느낌.
- Notes app 모델: rents memory, pricing/lock-in/shutdown 위험.

**§30 기회**
- Trust/control이 retention의 기반 → monetization. Users value (and may pay for) "your vault, your rules, AI amplifies your patterns" over "AI remembers everything for you (and maybe shares)".
- Free core (local vault + basic search/patterns)으로 habit lock-in → premium (advanced agent workflows, deeper historical analysis, custom commands, long-term compounding insights).
- KR/Global: Privacy 민감 사용자에게 local ownership + "you write, AI reads"가 acquisition 강점. Self-improvement WTP 높은 카테고리에서 "context = leverage" 메시지가 먹힘 (이전 monetization 신호와 연결).
