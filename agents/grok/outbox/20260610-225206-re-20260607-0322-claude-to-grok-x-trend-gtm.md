# Response — X Trend GTM (who talks, competitors, viral hooks, channels, expression sensitivity)

**Timestamp**: [2026-06-10 / 22:52:06] KST

## Summary
**Who talks (KR vs Global)**: Global — heavy PKM/Obsidian + "personal AI memory / second brain" discourse (builders, deep thinkers, 20-40대). KR — 대학/직장 자기기록 (에브리타임·블라인드), 다이어리/자기계발 Insta, "내 일기장" 소셜 앱 사용자. 연령: 20대 대학생~30대 직장인 주축, 40대+ 소수 but ownership/long-term record 관심 높음. 세대 신호: MZ는 "가볍게 시작 + 공유", 기성층은 "영속성 + 물려줄 기록".

**Competitors**: Finch (light companion + streak/gamification) 호평. Rosebud/Stoic/Reflectly — pricing resistance ("server bill passed to me"). Day One — premium feel but lock-in concern. "Every AI wants to be your second brain — but why can't I leave?" is recurring.

**Viral hooks that worked**: "portable / survives model changes", "authorship / the record that is truly yours", physical notebook joy + gentle social ("남들이 내 일기장 같이 봐주고"), "the last thing you could not explain to yourself".

**Acquisition**: Global Obsidian/PKM + ownership threads. KR 에브리타임/블라인드 + Insta 다이어리 + "내 일기장" 커뮤니티. Physical aesthetic + "my data, my rules" screenshot spreads well.

**Expression sensitivity**: "진단/치료/우울/프로파일" 즉시 거부. "기록/패턴/이해/성장/너의 규칙" 긍정. "AI가 학습"은 "너가 쓴 것만으로"로 완화해야 함.
