Core Names — Signals + Recommendations

Observed: "Second brain" carries persistent fatigue ("filing tax", "you don't need a second brain app, you need a first filter"). "Soul Core" / grand cores risk spiritual or heavy first impression. "Pattern" terms neutral in KR growth contexts but benefit from dynamic verbs ("연결고리", "흐름", "weave").

Advisory: Keep hierarchy for architecture/docs. Consumer-facing: lead with "내 기록의 중심" or "패턴의 연결". Progressive reveal of Bond (관계의 연결), Wisdom (지혜의 흐름), Narrative (기록의 보관), Muse (영감의 흐름), Growth (성장의 설계). "Pattern Link" as "신호의 통로 / 살아있는 연결" has better share potential.

Character Names — Signals + Recommendations

  • Relia (관계/사랑): "따뜻한 길잡이" — strong. Warm, supportive, non-clinical. High trust/approachability signal.
  • Lumen (배움/지식): Light + knowledge association positive. "지혜의 등불" or "패턴 철학자" framing works; avoids clinical.
  • Iris (취향/영감; Lumina → Iris confirmed): Distinct from Lumen. Mythic-inspiration safe. "영감 큐레이터" or "균형 안내자" — good for lifestyle balance hooks.
  • Foreman momo / 모모 반장 (기록 보관소): Excellent KR-relatable. "분류·검색 반장" functional, friendly, village/office tone. Keep simple.
  • archon (일/성장): Authority/ruler connotation risk (cold or distant). Test "성장 설계자" / "커리어 길잡이" for warmer functional tone.

General: Users map companions to precise needs (comfort vs analysis vs inspiration). Specific role copy outperforms vague grand claims. "Persona" compression rejected — emphasize living/returning patterns.

Analytic / Divergent Mode Framing — Signals + Recommendations

Observed: Academic terms (analytic/divergent) create understanding tax. Users respond better to benefit-first practical language. DMN (inward self-reflection + creative insight) aligns divergent with natural creative state. Contrast demos ("this from my data" vs "fresh angle") support share/virality.

Recommended intuitive pairs (KR/EN, low-pressure):

  • 기록 모드 (Trace Mode) — 내 데이터로 분석·정리
  • 스파크 모드 (Spark Mode) — 기록에서 새 가능성 탐색
  • 또는 내 길 모드 vs 넓은 시야 모드

Example surface copy: "기록으로 나를 들여다보거나, 기록 너머 새 각도를 발견하세요. 언제든 전환."

Avoid psych/academic jargon on first exposure. Goal: instant grasp of difference + easy demo for retention/share.
