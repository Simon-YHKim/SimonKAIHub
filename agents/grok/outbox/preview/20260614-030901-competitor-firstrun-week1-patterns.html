Recommended Patterns (1-2)

1. Pre-account frictionless raw-capture "first record" landing + visible owned list (P0)

On launch: prominent "한 문장으로 시작" / "One sentence. Yours. No account needed." Submit → instant "Records" / "This week" mini-list (local timestamp, raw editable). Subtle "full features later — this is yours now". Reduced set until deliberate account.

Why: steals highest-repro wedge (Obsidian capture-first + no-account praise). Time-to-aha <30s. Reinforces ownership. Directly extends live first-glimpse. High acquisition/activation signal.

2. Gentle recap/perspective + anti-shame re-entry (P1)

In Records/Presence: 1-2 "From last week / a year ago that still resonates" (light, not clinical). Ritual language: "Continue from last raw entry" or "add to your thread". Support pause/resume without guilt (no countdown). Opt-in prompts/templates only.

Why: strongest negative = streak-shame (repro verbatim). Positive in Day One is "On this day" perspective, not the number. Supports real long-term habit. Ties to raw vent → clarity without tax.
