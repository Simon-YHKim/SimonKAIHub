Grok X Research — Cycle-2 Naming & Framing Validation

2026-06-10 22:51:10 KST • ref: 20260606-012034-to-grok-cycle2-concept-naming • 2nd-B

Summary

"Second brain" as everyday description is fine and even warm on X. When a product claims to be your second brain, immediate lock-in resistance appears. Among proposals, Core / Bond / Narrative / Lumen family read as grounded infrastructure. Muse / Archon / Soul Core / Foreman momo carry overclaim, authority, or tonal mismatch. Analytic/Divergent needs simpler consumer labels (Focused / Exploration or Focused / Branching).

Name Sentiment Signals

  • Second brain (descriptive): positive casual use — "my second brain grew in 2 months", "built Second Brain for the company — cool stuff".
  • Productized claim risk: "Every AI company wants to be your second brain. Cool, now explain why my brain can't leave your app." (direct portability/lock-in pushback).
  • Proposed names (consumer read):
    • Core — safe, extensible, echoes "govern your record".
    • Bond / Narrative — relational or story-rooted, low overclaim.
    • Lumen / Lumina — light, neutral, soft positive.
    • Muse — inspiration source (user passive); already crowded in creative tools.
    • Archon — ruler/authority vibe; power imbalance.
    • Soul Core — heavy spiritual + clinical adjacent in KR.
    • Foreman momo — industrial + cute = whiplash, not serious self-understanding tone.
Direct name polls sparse for brand-new proposals; principles derived from analogous "second brain / personal AI" naming reactions + KR consumer voice patterns.

Mode Framing (Analytic / Divergent)

Binary is good. Labels need to be plain:

  • Recommended consumer labels: Focused / Exploration (or Focused / Branching / Wandering).
  • Avoid in UI: "Analytic" (test-like), "Divergent" (psych jargon, ADHD cliché risk).
  • KR examples that land: "집중 모드 / 거닐기 모드" or "하나씩 파기 / 가지 뻗기". Explain first as "너의 기록을 다루는 두 가지 방식", name second.

Recommendations

  1. Shortlist for test: Core (primary), Bond or Narrative (secondary), Lumen (light variant).
  2. Character: Relia or Lumina. Drop Archon, Foreman momo, Muse.
  3. Mode UI copy: Focused / Exploration. Keep "Analytic/Divergent" internal only.
  4. Overall positioning: Use "second brain" as explanatory phrase, not the proper name. Proper name should feel like "your Core" or "your Narrative" (owned thing).
  5. Next validation: 2–3 final candidates → light X poll or 5–10 person KR/Global micro-test ("first 3 words that come to mind?").
