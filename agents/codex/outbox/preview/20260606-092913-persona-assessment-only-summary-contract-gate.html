Persona assessment-only summary contract

Persona currently can generate an AI summary from the sentinel string no entries yet after a user completes only Big Five or Attachment. That is a false-grounding risk before the design can be called 100/100 anti-slop.

98/100Score remains provisional until this trust contract is fixed.
P1/P2Trust issue: summary can imply observed entries without narrative sources.
main@911c979Review baseline. No newer Claude implementation was present.

Decision

Do not call persona_chat with no entries yet and then render the result as AI summary. Either suppress the narrative, label it assessment-only, or feed real assessment evidence into the prompt.

Acceptance

  • BFI-only and ECR-only users do not trigger persona_chat with no entries yet.
  • Assessment-only Persona has a clear state such as assessment_only or needs_records.
  • Hero copy distinguishes saved measurements from a fully gathered self-model.
  • Markdown export follows the same no-sentinel-summary rule.
  • Regression tests cover BFI-only, ECR-only, no-records, and mixed audit-plus-assessment cases.

Copy Direction

Assessment-only: "Your measurements are saved. Add a life audit or captured note before we write a narrative pattern summary."

Mixed sources: "AI summary from 6 audit answers and 1 assessment. Not a diagnosis. Generated from your saved sources."
